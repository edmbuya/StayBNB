Privacy & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Privacy Policy

At Compassview, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1.1 Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you make a booking inquiry or reservation through our website.

1.2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to process bookings, respond to inquiries, send confirmations, and improve our services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1.3 Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

2. Refund Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. Our refund policy is outlined below to ensure clarity for all guests.

2.1 Cancellation Window

Reservations cancelled at least 7 days before the scheduled check-in date are eligible for a full refund, minus any applicable processing fees.

2.2 Late Cancellations

Cancellations made within 7 days of check-in may be eligible for a partial refund (50%) at our discretion, depending on circumstances.

2.3 No-Shows

Guests who do not arrive for their reservation without prior notice are not eligible for a refund.

2.4 How to Request a Refund

To request a cancellation or refund, please contact us directly through the contact information provided on our website as soon as possible.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy & Refund Policy, please reach out to us through our contact section.
